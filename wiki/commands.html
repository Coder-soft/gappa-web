Available Bot Commands

Here is a list of commands you can use with the bot, along with their descriptions and examples.

!help

Description: Displays a list of all available commands.

Usage: `!help`

!fetch [youtube link]

Description: Fetches Video information from a given youtube video.

Usage: `!fetch https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=`

!check [song title or YouTube URL]

Description: Checks the copyright status of a song or YouTube video.

Usage: `!check https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=/Song name`

!thumb [YouTube URL]

Description: Get the HD thumbnail of a YouTube video.

Usage: `!thumb https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=`

!youtube [channel ID]

Description: Get detailed statistics for a YouTube channel.

Usage: `!youtube UCMNP1K_uUp8xPKl7HqcRi9A`

!getid [YouTube handle]

Description: Get the channel ID for a given YouTube handle (with or without '@') and provide a quick option to view channel stats.

Usage: `!getid @Gappa`
